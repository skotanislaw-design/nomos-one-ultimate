## Getting Started

### Step 1: Receive Your Portal Code

Your lawyer will send you an email with a unique portal code. The email will contain:
- Your name
- Case details
- **A unique access code** (12-character code)

### Step 2: Log In to Your Portal

1. Go to: `https://nomos.skotanislaw.com/portal/login`
2. Enter your **full name** (as provided)
3. Select your **case category**
4. Enter the **portal code** from your email
5. Click **Είσοδος** (Login)

**Important**: The portal code is personal and confidential. Do not share it with anyone.

### Step 3: Verify Your Information

After successful login, you'll see your case dashboard with:
- Case title and number
- Case status
- Your lawyer's contact information
- Fees and balance information

### 4. Send a Message

**How to Contact Your Lawyer**:

1. Find the **"Επικοινωνία με Δικηγόρο"** (Contact Lawyer) section
2. Type your message
3. Click **"Αποστολή Μηνύματος"** (Send Message)

**Best Practices**:
- Be clear and specific
- Include relevant case details
- Expect response within 1-2 business days
- Keep messages professional

**Example Messages**:
- "Έχω ερώτηση σχετικά με την πρόοδο της υπόθεσης"
  (I have a question about the case progress)
- "Θα ήθελα να ανεβάσω νέα έγγραφα"
  (I would like to upload new documents)

---

### 5. Upload Documents

**How to Upload Files**:

1. Find the **"Ανέβασμα Εγγράφων"** (Upload Documents) section
2. Click the upload area or drag & drop files
3. Select the file to upload
4. Click **"Ανέβασμα"** (Upload)
5. Confirm upload success message

**Allowed File Types**:
- PDF files (.pdf)
- Word documents (.doc, .docx)
- Excel spreadsheets (.xls, .xlsx)
- Images (.jpg, .png)
- Text files (.txt)

**File Requirements**:
- Maximum size: 50 MB
- One file at a time
- Clear, readable content

**Tips**:
- Scan documents at 300 DPI for clarity
- Use PDF format for best compatibility
- Name files clearly (e.g., "Invoice_April_2026.pdf")
- Keep file size under 5 MB when possible

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Login Issues

**Q: I forgot my portal code**
A: Contact your lawyer or the office. You cannot reset your code online.

**Q: I'm getting "Μη έγκυρος κωδικός πρόσβασης" (Invalid access code)**
A: 
- Check if you typed the code correctly (spaces and case matter)
- Verify your name matches exactly as provided
- Select the correct case category
- Contact your lawyer if the code was provided incorrectly

**Q: My session timed out**
A: You'll need to log in again. Sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity for security.

---

### Messages & Communication

**Q: How long until my lawyer responds?**
A: Usually within 1-2 business days. Urgent matters may be prioritized.

**Q: Can I call my lawyer directly?**
A: Yes! Contact information is displayed on your dashboard.

**Q: Are my messages private?**
A: Yes. Messages are encrypted and only visible to your lawyer and admin staff.

---

### Document Uploads

**Q: What happens after I upload a document?**
A: Your lawyer is notified. They will review and may request clarification.

**Q: Can I upload multiple documents?**
A: Yes, but one at a time. You can upload as many documents as needed.

**Q: How long should I keep copies?**
A: Keep copies until your case is completely resolved.

**Q: What if I upload the wrong file?**
A: Contact your lawyer to request removal and reupload the correct file.

---

### Fees & Payments

**Q: Why has my balance not updated?**
A: Payments may take 1-2 business days to process and appear in the system.

**Q: How can I make a payment?**
A: Contact your lawyer for payment instructions (bank transfer, check, etc.).

**Q: Can I see itemized fees?**
A: Ask your lawyer for an itemized invoice if you need details.

**Q: What if I disagree with the charges?**
A: Contact your lawyer immediately to discuss the fees.

---

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Is my information secure?**
A: Yes. The portal uses encryption and security best practices.

**Q: Who can see my information?**
A: Only your assigned lawyer and office staff can access your case.

**Q: Can I download documents I've uploaded?**
A: Contact your lawyer if you need copies of your own documents.

**Q: What happens to my data after the case ends?**
A: Your data is retained per legal requirements and then securely deleted.

## Troubleshooting

### I Can't Log In

**Step 1**: Verify you're at the correct URL
- `https://nomos.skotanislaw.com/portal/login`

**Step 2**: Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

**Step 3**: Clear your browser cache
- Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows/Linux)
- Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)

**Step 4**: Try a different device
- Use your phone, tablet, or different computer

**Step 5**: Contact your lawyer
- They can verify your access code

---

### The Page Won't Load

**Try these steps**:

1. **Check your internet connection**
   - Test by visiting another website
   - Try connecting to a different WiFi network

2. **Disable browser extensions**
   - Some extensions block content
   - Try "incognito" or "private browsing" mode

3. **Update your browser**
   - Use the latest version of your browser

4. **Check system time**
   - Your device time must be correct
   - Sync with internet time

5. **Try a different browser**
   - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge

---

### Messages Not Sending

**Check these items**:

1. **Internet connection** - Active and stable
2. **Message length** - Not excessively long
3. **Text format** - No special characters causing issues
4. **Browser cache** - Clear and refresh
5. **Contact your lawyer** if problem persists

---

### Files Won't Upload

**Verify**:

1. **File size** - Under 50 MB
2. **File type** - Supported format (PDF, DOC, XLS, JPG, PNG, TXT)
3. **File name** - No special characters
4. **Disk space** - You have space available
5. **Internet** - Stable connection
6. **Browser** - Latest version

## Security Tips

### Protect Your Account

✓ **Do**:
- Keep your portal code confidential
- Use a strong password if prompted
- Log out when finished
- Use secure WiFi (avoid public WiFi for sensitive actions)
- Keep your email address current

✗ **Don't**:
- Share your login code with anyone
- Use the same password as your email
- Leave your account open unattended
- Click suspicious links in emails
- Download attachments from unknown sources

### Recognize Phishing

Be alert for fake emails claiming to be from:
- "Nomos One Admin"
- "Account Security Team"
- "Verify Your Account"

**Real emails from us will**:
- Come from official domain
- Not ask for your password
- Not ask for your portal code
- Contain your case details
